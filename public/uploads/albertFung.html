Albert Fung (he, him) b. 1970, San Francisco, CA

Albert Fung’s work is meaningful when it rings like a bell, shocks like the new, and bothers like memory. It is like remembering what you forgot you never knew. His work is kinesthetic and synaesthetic: a sensation of color leaves a taste in one’s mouth; pictorial rhythms land like body blows.

He has been producing artwork in oil, acrylic, watercolor, pastel, intaglio, lithography and digital imaging for over thirty years. He attended Rochester Institute of Technology (BFA-Printmaking) and Massachusetts College of Art (MFA-Painting and Printmaking).

He has shown throughout Philadelphia; —— including LGTripp Gallery, Roger LaPelle Galleries, Boston Street Gallery, and City Art Salon —— and New York —— including Art-O-Mat Gallery, OZANEAUX, Ltd, Climate Gallery, and Laumont Editions Gallery.

He lives and works in Philadelphia. His work can be seen at alfung.com (IG: @smudgypics)
