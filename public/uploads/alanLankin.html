Alan Lankin (he, him), b. Vineland, NJ

Alan Lankin constructs painting and works on paper. He studied art at the University of Pennsylvania and the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts has had a residency at the Vermont Studio Center. He has had solo shows in Pennsylvania and New Jersey and has exhibited his paintings and works on paper in group shows at PAFA and the Woodmere Art Museum in Philadelphia, as well Salus University and West Chester University in Pennsylvania. He lives and works in the Philadelphia area.
