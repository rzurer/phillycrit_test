Robert Zurer (he, him), b. 1948, New York, NY

Zurerâ€™s childhood friends were the offspring of parents active in the creative community of Greenwich Village in the 1950s. He started drawing at a young age, when he and his father played a game wherein his father scribbled some lines on paper and asked him to make a drawing out of them. Decades later, this kind of automatic drawing remains a starting point for many of his paintings.

Zurer is primarily self-taught. Zurer has been an active member of the NYC Crit Club since its inception in 2017. In 2022 he was accepted into the White Columns Curated Artist Registry and in 2023 he was a resident at the Vermont Studio Center.

In 2018, in order to be able to paint full time, Zurer quit his day job, leased his loft in Brooklyn and moved to Philadelphia.
