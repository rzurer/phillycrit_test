Please read the following community guidelines.

For Attendees and Presenters:

  • Please review the mission and core values of Philly Crit before attending or presenting.
  • Philly Crit reserves the right to use images of participants and/or their work for promotional purposes on its website, Instagram, Facebook and email.

For Attendees:

  • Be respectful of presenters as well as other attendees.
  • Be mindful of listening without interrupting. When offering constructive feedback, refrain from using harsh, rude, or disrespectful language. Be aware of the 25-minute time limit and allow others to speak if you have already provided feedback.
  • Silence your phone for the duration of the crit session. If you need to take a call, please step outside the crit room.

For Presenters:

  • Arrive before the beginning of the session to set up your work and remove it at the end of the crit.
  • Be open to constructive feedback on your work and respectful of fellow artists’ work.
  • Be mindful of the 25-minute time limit.
  • If you cannot present, please notify Philly Crit via email at least 48 hours prior to the crit.
  • The venue will provide supplies and hardware for hanging your work. If you have special requirements for your presentation, please email Philly Crit at least 48 hours before the crit date.

Please email us at admin@phillycrit.com if you have questions about any of our policies.
