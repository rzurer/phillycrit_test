We are happy to announce that we will be holding Philly Crit Meeting #26 which is being generously hosted by PAFA.

Date: Sunday, September 14, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - Noon

Place: PAFA, 128 N.Broad Street Philly


Philly Crit provides a free monthly space for Philadelphia-based artists to come together to share and discuss artwork. We will foster an atmosphere of constructive peer-to-peer criticism free of judgment and hierarchy.

To attend this crit you must have registered and signed up. If you have not already registered with our site, please do so by clicking here. If you have already registered and want to attend, please go to the signup area of our site.

If you have already signed up but at a later date, find that you no longer can attend, it is important that you please let us know by logging on to the site to make that change so that others can have your spot. We also ask that you attend at least one crit before signing up to present.
